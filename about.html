About Me

Hi, I’m Priya Modi (Dain) — a biomedical communicator and medical illustrator based in Miami, FL. I specialize in transforming complex science into clear, visually engaging, and inclusive media. My work spans 2D illustration, 3D modelling, animation, and interactive UX/UI design for healthcare, biotech, and education. I help clients tell stories that resonate with diverse audiences while maintaining scientific accuracy.

Outside of work, I enjoy exploring new cities with my husband, experimenting in the kitchen, and browsing local bookshops for my next great read. On weekends, you’ll often find me discovering Miami’s vibrant art and design scene.
